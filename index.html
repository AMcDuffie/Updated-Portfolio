About Me

Arlette McDuffie is a Graphic Artist and a Full stack Web Developer, with over 25 years of work experience. Arlette works well with large and small IT teams. She partners with her peer technologists to provide hands-on assistance while sharing knowledge of tools and techniques with both developers and non-developers.

In her current position, Arlette provides weekly progress reports to management and other affected stakeholders, collaborating to form firm view of quality metrics and expectations for the delivery of web based applications, as well as working with other IT developers as each task is documented throughout a bi-weekly sprint. Arlette provides technical advice and assists in solving programming problems with a strong working knowledge of development techniques and methodologies, and recommendations towards the development of new code or to reuse of existing code to develop modern web frameworks and tools.

Arlette continues to increase her skills and training to provide input on continual development of cutting edge tools with a view that provides tangible business benefit. Arlette is an expertise in Web development using JavaScript/jQuery, CSS, front-end JS Frameworks and HTML.
